About Me

I started my journey in the digital world with a fascination for tech and web development. But somewhere along the line, the focus shifted – I became captivated by the user experience and the visual language that brings it to life. Now, I wear two hats: freelance UX/UI designer and graphic designer. I love crafting intuitive interfaces and visually compelling experiences, big or small.

Outside of design, you'll find me curled up with a good book. African fiction holds a special place on my shelf – the stories are bursting with vibrancy and tell a unique side of the human experience. It's a constant source of inspiration, both for design and for life in general.
